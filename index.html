The State of Immigration

“Remember, remember always, that all of us, you and I especially, are descended from immigrants and revolutionists.”
Franklin D. Roosevelt

The history of America is a history of immigration. The country is founded on generations of people from around the world, bringing their stories to the United States to live and work.

In today's polarized political climate, the beauty of American immigration is often forgotten. Too little attention is paid to the fact that every person who migrates to the US is a vital part of the living history of the country.

This visualization project explores trends in work visa immigration from a contemporary lens- 2007 to 2017. We look at where people have come from, the history, who they are, where they move, and how the 2006 election has impacted work immigration.

We hope this data art project can combat xenophobia and remind us of the beauty and value of migration.
